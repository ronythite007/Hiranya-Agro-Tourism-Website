Discover Our Hilltop Haven

Breathtaking Location & Scenic Viewse

Nestled at the top of a hill, our resort offers unrivaled panoramic views of majestic mountains and lush greenery. The serene surroundings provide the perfect backdrop for relaxation, while the fresh mountain air rejuvenates your senses. Whether you’re seeking tranquility or adventure, this hilltop retreat has it all.

Our specially designed octagonal-shaped huts provide a one-of-a-kind experience, blending nature and luxury seamlessly.
